Ann Tseng's Hypertext Story: Conclusion

Growing up I moved around quite a bit.
I was born in Taiwan and spend my childhood travelling between Taiwan, the U.S., & Canada.
My family and I lived in Sammamish, Washington when I was 1 to 5 years old.
I went back and forth between Taiwan and the United States during that time.
Moving from place to place has always been a bittersweet experience to me...


I love learning while being immersed in other cultures, but I have always been a nostalgic person that loves the past.
Now I have come back to the U.S. for university...
and I realized that even though I've moved around a lot, my diverse experiences is something
that I will always carry with me regardless of where I may be heading off to next.
